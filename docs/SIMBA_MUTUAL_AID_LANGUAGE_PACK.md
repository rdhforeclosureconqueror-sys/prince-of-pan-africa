# Simba Mutual Aid Language Pack

**Version:** 0.1  
**Status:** Member-facing copy and communication guide  
**Purpose:** Provide approved language for Simba Mutual Aid Society platform screens, emails, notifications, admin decisions, public pages, and community updates.

## Core Message

The Simba Mutual Aid Society is a governed community support system.

Members may contribute, request support in a later approved phase, nominate others in a later approved phase, and help guide community priorities. Aid is reviewed under written policy and distributed based on need, available funds, eligibility, documentation, and approval.

Short version:

> The Simba Mutual Aid Society helps members organize care, emergency support, preparedness, and community development through a governed community fund.

## Mutual Aid Overview Page Copy

### Page Title

Simba Mutual Aid Society

### Short Subtitle

A governed community support system for care, preparedness, and resilience.

### Main Body Copy

The Simba Mutual Aid Society helps members support one another through organized community care.

Members may contribute to the fund, request support in a later approved phase, nominate another member in a later approved phase, and help guide community priorities. Requests are reviewed under written policy and considered based on need, available funds, eligibility, documentation, and approval.

Mutual aid support is not guaranteed. The fund is not a personal member balance or cash-equivalent wallet.

### Activation Copy

The Mutual Aid Society is currently **Building Toward Activation**. It begins live aid review only after the designated Mutual Aid / Community Development Fund reaches **$20,000** in available or committed funding and after approved policy, governance process, accounting controls, privacy rules, and approval controls are in place.

Until activation, this page shows the purpose, progress, rules, and launch path. No mutual aid distributions occur before activation.

### Fund Progress Label

Use: Mutual Aid Fund Progress.

Do not use labels that imply individual ownership, member cash access, or personal shares.

## Request Aid Page Copy

### Page Title

Request Mutual Aid Support

### Intro Copy

This request flow is not active yet. If a future phase approves it, members may use the form to request support from the Simba Mutual Aid Society.

Requests are reviewed by authorized reviewers under written policy. Support is based on need, available funds, eligibility, documentation, and approval. Submitting a request does not guarantee support.

### Consent Checkbox

I understand that mutual aid support is not guaranteed. I agree that authorized Simba Mutual Aid reviewers may review the information I submit. I understand that false or incomplete information may delay review or result in non-approval.

### Documentation Notice

Some requests may require documentation, such as a bill, notice, invoice, receipt, or other proof of need. If more information is needed, the review team will contact you.

### Preferred Support Method Label

Use: Preferred support method.

Options: pay provider/vendor directly, reimbursement after receipt if approved by policy, direct member support if allowed by policy, voucher or restricted support, or other.

Avoid labels that imply cash-equivalent access, automatic approval, or guaranteed payment.

## Nominate Someone Page Copy

### Page Title

Nominate a Member for Support

### Intro Copy

Use this form, if a future phase enables it, to let the Mutual Aid Society know that another member may need support.

A nomination does not guarantee aid. The nominated member may need to confirm their request, provide information, and agree to the mutual aid review process.

### Consent Warning

Please share only respectful and necessary information. Do not upload private documents for another person unless they have given permission.

## Member Notifications

### Request Submitted

Your mutual aid request has been submitted. The review team will check eligibility, documentation, available funds, and category fit. Support is not guaranteed, and we may contact you if more information is needed.

### More Information Needed

More information is needed to continue reviewing your mutual aid request. Please upload the requested information or contact the support team if you need help.

### Approved

Your mutual aid request has been approved. The approved support amount and method will be shown in your request details. Some support may be paid directly to a provider or vendor.

### Not Approved

Your mutual aid request was not approved at this time. This may be due to eligibility, documentation, category fit, available funds, request limits, or other policy requirements. You may be able to appeal if the decision notice says an appeal is available.

### Support Completed

Your approved mutual aid support has been completed. Please upload any required receipt or follow-up confirmation if requested.

## Denial Reason Language

Use respectful reason labels:

```text
Eligibility requirement not met
Request outside approved categories
More documentation needed
Fund availability limit
Request frequency limit
Unable to verify information
Policy requirement not met
Duplicate or overlapping request
```

Avoid shaming, accusatory, or speculative labels. For suspected abuse, say: This request requires additional review before a decision can be made.

## Appeal Copy

### Appeal Page Intro

Use this form to ask for a second review of your mutual aid decision. Appeals are available when information may have been misunderstood, documentation is now available, eligibility status may be incorrect, or a conflict of interest may have affected the review.

### Appeal Submitted

Your appeal has been submitted. A different authorized reviewer or governance representative may review the decision. Appeal review does not guarantee a different outcome.

## Impact Report Copy

### Public Impact Header

Mutual Aid Impact

### Public Impact Summary

Together, members and supporters are building a community care system rooted in dignity, preparedness, and shared responsibility.

### Safe Metrics

Use total contributed, total distributed after activation, members supported, requests approved, average review time, categories supported, fund reserve, and community projects supported.

### Safe Story Format

Use anonymized stories, such as: a Simba member received emergency transportation support after a family crisis; a local preparedness project received support for supplies and training; several families received short-term food and household support during a difficult month.

Do not identify recipients unless they gave explicit written consent.

## Admin Decision Language

Admins and reviewers should write notes that are factual, respectful, minimal, policy-based, and free of insults or assumptions.

Good example: Member meets active-status requirement. Documentation confirms utility shutoff notice. Request fits emergency relief category. Recommend approval for $225 direct support to provider.

Bad example: They always have problems and probably need to budget better.

## Public FAQ

### What is the Simba Mutual Aid Society?

It is a governed community support system that helps members organize care, emergency support, preparedness, and community development.

### Is support guaranteed?

No. Support is reviewed under written policy and depends on need, eligibility, documentation, approval, committee review, and available funds.

### Is Mutual Aid active now?

No. The Mutual Aid Society is Building Toward Activation.

### What must happen before activation?

The $20,000 threshold must be reached, and approved policy, governance process, accounting controls, privacy rules, and approval controls must be in place.

### Can members apply now?

No. There is no member-facing application flow yet.

### Are live payouts authorized?

No. Live payouts are not authorized in Phase 1.

### Who decides requests?

Authorized reviewers, committee members, admins, and governance roles review requests according to written policy after activation and later phase approval.

### Can I nominate someone?

Only if nominations are enabled in a later approved phase. A nomination does not guarantee support, and the nominated member may need to confirm information.

## Required Activation and Safety Guardrails

- Mutual Aid Society is currently **Building Toward Activation**.
- The **$20,000 threshold** must be reached before activation.
- Activation also requires approved policy, governance process, accounting controls, privacy rules, and approval controls.
- There are **no mutual aid distributions before activation**.
- There is **no automatic approval**.
- There are **no guaranteed aid promises**.
- There is **no cash-equivalent wallet balance**.
- There is **no runtime fund movement in this PR**.
- There is **no member-facing application flow yet**.
- There is **no payment, payout, or reimbursement logic in this PR**.
- Live payouts are not authorized by this documentation package.
- Support is reviewed under policy and depends on need, available funds, eligibility, documentation, approval, and committee review.
