# Simba Mutual Aid Language Pack

**Status:** Approved language planning for Phase 1 documentation only.

## Core Message

Simba Mutual Aid Society is Building Toward Activation. It is preparing governed community support through policy, governance, accounting controls, privacy rules, and approval controls. Support will be reviewed and limited by available funds after activation; it is not guaranteed.

## Approved Words

Use governed community support, reviewed support, community care, available funds, eligibility, documentation, approval, committee review, impact reporting, activation threshold, and Building Toward Activation.

## Words to Avoid

Avoid terms that imply automatic support, financial product behavior, member cash access, personal accumulation, or guaranteed outcomes. Prefer plain community-care language that explains review, limits, and governance.

## Overview Page Copy

Community Mutual Aid Society is Building Toward Activation. The Society activates only after the community reaches the $20,000 threshold and after approved policy, governance process, accounting controls, privacy rules, and approval controls are in place. There are no mutual aid distributions before activation.

## Request Aid Page Copy

This page is not active yet. A member-facing application flow will only be considered in a later approved phase after activation requirements are complete. Submitting information, if a future pilot is approved, will not guarantee support.

## Nominate Someone Page Copy

Nominations are not active in Phase 1. If a future nomination process is approved, it must protect privacy, avoid pressure, and follow eligibility and committee review rules.

## Request Status Labels

Draft labels: Submitted, Needs information, Under committee review, Conflict review in progress, Finance review in progress, Approved for manual processing, Not approved, Appealed, Closed.

## Notifications

- Receipt: We received your request for reviewed support. This confirmation does not mean approval.
- Missing information: More documentation is needed before review can continue.
- Review: Your request is under committee review.
- Decision: The committee has completed review under the current policy.

## Denial Reason Language

Use respectful language: The request could not be approved under the current policy and available funds. Provide a clear category reason when appropriate, such as outside current category, documentation incomplete, policy limit reached, or committee unable to approve at this time.

## Approval Template

The committee approved your request for manual processing under the current policy. Finance controls must be completed before any support is processed.

## Not-Approved Template

The committee is not able to approve this request under the current policy. This decision does not judge your worth or your place in the community. If policy allows, you may request reconsideration.

## Appeal Copy

You may ask for reconsideration if you believe information was missed or policy was applied incorrectly. Appeals are reviewed by authorized non-conflicted reviewers and do not guarantee approval.

## Impact Report Copy

This report shares aggregate Mutual Aid Society activity while protecting member privacy. It summarizes reviewed requests, support categories, available funds, policy changes, and lessons learned.

## Admin Decision Language

Admins and reviewers should write concise reasons tied to policy, documentation, category fit, available funds, conflicts, and accounting controls. Avoid shaming or speculative commentary.

## FAQ

### Is Mutual Aid active now?

No. It is Building Toward Activation.

### What must happen before activation?

The $20,000 threshold must be reached, and approved policy, governance process, accounting controls, privacy rules, and approval controls must be in place.

### Is support guaranteed?

No. Support is reviewed, limited by available funds, and subject to eligibility, documentation, approval, and committee review.

### Can members apply now?

No. There is no member-facing application flow yet.

### Are live payouts authorized?

No. Live payouts are not authorized in Phase 1.

## Required Activation Guardrails

- Mutual Aid Society is currently **Building Toward Activation**.
- The **$20,000 threshold** must be reached before activation.
- Activation also requires approved policy, governance process, accounting controls, privacy rules, and approval controls.
- There are **no mutual aid distributions before activation**.
- Support is reviewed support, not guaranteed support.
- There is no automatic approval.
- There is no cash-equivalent member balance.
- There is no runtime fund movement in this Phase 1 documentation PR.
- There is no member-facing application flow yet.
- There is no payment, payout, or reimbursement logic in this PR.
