# Simba Mutual Aid Society Operating Appendix

**Status:** Human operating rules for Phase 1 documentation only.

## Mutual Aid Society Charter

The Society exists to organize governed community support for members facing urgent destabilizing needs after activation. It operates through care, discipline, privacy, records, and accountability.

## Mutual Aid Committee Charter

The Committee reviews eligible requests under approved policy, documents decisions, manages conflicts, protects privacy, and reports aggregate results to governance.

## Conflict-of-Interest Policy

Reviewers must disclose personal, family, business, financial, romantic, supervisory, or close organizational ties that could affect judgment. Conflicted reviewers must recuse, and recusals must be recorded.

## Aid Request Application Template

Future template fields: member identity reference, contact preference, support category, urgency, amount or type requested if allowed by policy, short need summary, documentation checklist, privacy acknowledgment, non-guarantee acknowledgment, and consent for committee review.

## Review Rubric

Review factors: activation status, eligibility, category fit, urgency, documentation, available funds, prior support history, safety concerns, conflict review, policy limits, and accounting readiness.

## Appeals Process

A member may request reconsideration under approved policy. Appeals should be time-bounded, respectful, reviewed by non-conflicted reviewers, and documented. Appeals do not guarantee approval.

## Emergency Aid Procedure

Emergency procedures require pre-approved limits, at least two authorized reviewers when possible, immediate conflict screening, finance confirmation, post-decision documentation, and governance reporting. No emergency procedure is active before activation.

## Accounting and Fund Controls

Controls include separate fund tracking, source classification, approval references, dual review, reconciliation, correction records, monthly review, retention rules, and audit-ready exports.

## Fraud, Abuse, and Safety Policy

Potential fraud, coercion, retaliation, harassment, or unsafe behavior must be escalated under approved safety procedures. Reviews should protect dignity while preserving fund integrity.

## Policy Versioning

Every policy change needs version number, approval date, approving body, change summary, effective date, and archival of prior versions.

## Notifications

Notifications should confirm receipt, request missing information, communicate status, communicate decisions respectfully, and explain appeals. Notifications must not promise approval.

## Service-Level Targets

Draft targets: receipt confirmation within two business days, initial completeness review within five business days, committee decision within ten business days when documentation is complete, emergency review faster if approved by policy.

## Admin SOP

Admins verify access, log requests, assign reviewers, check conflicts, maintain records, send approved language, coordinate finance checklist completion, and prepare aggregate reports.

## MVP Database Additions

Planning-only additions: policy_versions, aid_requests, request_documents, review_assignments, decision_records, recusal_records, accounting_checklists, manual_support_records, appeal_records, and aggregate_reports. No migrations are included in Phase 1.

## MVP Launch Checklist

- $20,000 threshold reached.
- Policy approved.
- Governance process approved.
- Accounting controls approved.
- Privacy rules approved.
- Approval controls approved.
- Conflict policy approved.
- Language reviewed.
- Reviewer training complete.
- Manual controls tested.
- Go/no-go decision recorded.

## Required Activation Guardrails

- Mutual Aid Society is currently **Building Toward Activation**.
- The **$20,000 threshold** must be reached before activation.
- Activation also requires approved policy, governance process, accounting controls, privacy rules, and approval controls.
- There are **no mutual aid distributions before activation**.
- Support is reviewed support, not guaranteed support.
- There is no automatic approval.
- There is no cash-equivalent member balance.
- There is no runtime fund movement in this Phase 1 documentation PR.
- There is no member-facing application flow yet.
- There is no payment, payout, or reimbursement logic in this PR.
